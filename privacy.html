1. Tool usage

Most tools on this site (image compressor, background remover, QR generator, PDF converter, Zakat calculator) run entirely in your browser. Files you upload are processed on your device and are not sent to our servers unless a specific tool states otherwise.

2. Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics (such as visit counts and page views) to understand which tools are useful and improve the site. This does not include reading the contents of files you process.

3. Advertising

This site may display ads through third-party providers (such as Google AdSense). These providers may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can control ad personalization through your browser or Google's Ads Settings.

4. Contact form

If you use the contact form, the information you submit (name, email, message) is used only to respond to your query and is not shared with third parties.

5. Your choices

  • You can clear your browser data at any time to remove any locally processed files.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can contact us any time to ask what data, if any, we hold about you.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site grows. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the contact page.
