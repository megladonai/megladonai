ABOUT US

Tools that get out of your way.

Megladon AI started with one simple frustration: too many "free" tools online demand a sign-up, hide behind a paywall after your third use, or upload your files to a server nobody explains. We wanted something faster and more honest than that.

Every tool on this site runs directly in your browser wherever possible — no account, no waiting, no watermarks. You open a tool, you use it, you're done. That's the whole idea.

What we stand for

Free, actually

No trial periods. No hidden paywalls after a few uses.

Privacy-first

Most tools process files locally in your browser — nothing gets uploaded.

No clutter

One tool, one job, done well. No bloated feature lists.

Who's behind it

Megladon AI is built and maintained independently, with new tools added based on what people actually search for and struggle to find good free versions of. Have a tool you wish existed? Get in touch — we're always adding to the toolbox.
